Electric Vehicle Charging Sessions Dundee
Every electric vehicle charging session at Dundee City Council owned charge points. 
Data includes:
 - Start and finish date/time, 
 - Kwh (amount of electricity) used, 
 - Charge point ID, 
 - Location, 
 - Type of Charger.

More information about electric vehicles in Dundee can be found on the Drive Dundee Electric website.
